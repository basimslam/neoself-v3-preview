04 · THE OPTIMIZED MAN

You measure everything else.

You know your resting heart rate. You know what you lifted last Tuesday. You read the back of the packet before you eat it. Then you buy skincare with no numbers on it at all, because nobody in this aisle will give you any. That was never your fault. It was the category's.

“Skincare isn’t for me.”
You already run a protocol for sleep, for training, for what you eat. This is the same thing for the organ that is on the outside. The only reason it felt like someone else’s aisle is that nobody in it was talking to you.
“I don’t have time for ten steps.”
Neither do we. Three products, one order, about ninety seconds a morning. There is no step four, and we are not planning one.
“I wouldn’t know what to buy.”
That is the point of a system. CLEAN, then REPAIR, then DEFEND. The order is on the box, and each one is formulated for the one after it. There is nothing to research.
“It’s all marketing.”
Usually, yes. So check ours. Every active is printed at the dose we used, the pH is a number, the full INCI is on the page, and every trial is published with its sample size, including the small ones. If we were bluffing, this is where you would catch us.
“What if it does nothing?”
Then the numbers will say so, and they are on the page before you buy. We would rather lose the sale than win it on a promise we cannot show you.
